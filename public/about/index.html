At Bengal Adventures Club

We believe adventure is not just about mountains - it's a lifestyle inside nature. Our mission is to help you break free from robotic routines and experience the thrill of the outdoors. From the Himalayan trails to the serene Dooars and the rugged terrains of Purulia, we bring you closer to nature in ways you never imagined.

Our Vision

To unite riders from all walks of life through a shared passion for the open road, brotherhood, and freedom. We strive to build a community that values loyalty, respect, and the spirit of adventure—riding not just for the destination, but for the journey that defines us.

  • Create a close-knit community of riders who seek unending adventure & Nature
  • Encourage people to taste the thrill of adventure tourism in Bengal
  • Promote sustainability through campaigns, eco-tourism, and awareness drives

Our Mission

To create a close-knit community of riders who seek unending adventure & Nature. Encourage people to taste the thrill of adventure tourism in Bengal.

  • Explore the unexplored: Trails, hikes, treks, and many more.
  • Embrace lifes best moments: Get a much-needed break from day to-day life
  • Be one with Nature: Enjoy the serenity of the mountains, forests, and hidden routes
  • Contribute to sustainability: Join our green group to foster eco friendly tourism in Bengal

Our Core Values

We ride with respect, loyalty, and integrity—upholding the true spirit of brotherhood. Freedom drives us, unity defines us, and every mile strengthens our bond.

  • Explore adventure routes and off-track rides
  • Encourage personal growth through challenges & curiosity
  • Generate the awareness of Nature preservation
  • Profit is never the goal, soul-stirring adventure is
